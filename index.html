About me

"Hi there! I'm deeply passionate about two things that fuel my creativity and imagination: anime and drawing. Anime, with its vibrant characters, rich storytelling, and diverse genres, has been a source of inspiration for me since I can remember. From the epic adventures of shonen series to the emotional depth of slice-of-life dramas, there's always something new to explore and appreciate. Drawing is my way of bringing the characters and worlds of anime to life in my own unique style. Whether it's sketching out my favorite characters or creating my own original designs, the process of putting pencil to paper allows me to express myself and delve deeper into the stories and themes that resonate with me.
